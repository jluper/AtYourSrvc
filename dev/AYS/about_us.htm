Sherry Luper

 

  
  Sherry has over 18 years experience in the Human Resources field.  She was in a Human Resources management position for a Big Five public accounting firm before joining a mid-size national company in Winston-Salem providing leadership for the Recruiting and Training functions in a 13-person Corporate Human Resources Department.  Sherry was very involved in the communication and implementation of a variety of corporate initiatives, including compensation programs, mentoring/counseling programs for high performers, and benefits programs that supported corporate objectives.    
   Sherry is committed to developing strong relationships with her clients based upon mutual respect, trust and integrity.   She is described as being a great facilitator who brings empathy and common sense to the table. 

Susan Raymer

 

  
   Susan has over 20 years of general business and  management experience; the last 10 years providing leadership for a corporate Human Resources Department with a mid-sized national company with corporate headquarters in Winston-Salem.  She was an integral member of the Senior Management team and partnered with them on many important strategic initiatives:  the acquisition of a national company; creation of several start-up companies; development of an Executive Compensation and Incentive Plan. 
   Susan is committed to providing exceptional client service and does so through offering practical, common sense advice that clients trust and rely upon.
